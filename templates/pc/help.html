What is 720Scores?

720Scores is a live football scores website providing up-to-the-minute scores, results, and statistics for football matches around the world.

How often are the scores updated?

Our scores are updated in real-time, ensuring you have the latest information on all football matches.

Do you cover all football leagues and competitions?

We cover a wide range of football leagues and competitions, including English Premier League, Bundlesliga, Spanish La Liga, Ligue 1, Italian seria A, club friendlies, World cup. We cover them all.

Can I access your website on my mobile device?

Yes, our website is fully responsive and accessible on all mobile devices, ensuring you can stay up-to-date with live scores wherever you are.

Do you offer any additional features or statistics?

Yes, we provide detailed statistics, including possession, shots on target, corners, Transfers. We also offer live commentary, News, venues, tables, fixtures & Match dayresults.

Is your website free to use?

Yes, our website is completely free to use, with no subscription or registration required to access our live scores and statistics.
