Privacy Policy for 720scores

Read the privacy policy for the world's number one destination for live scores for football.

Introduction

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from users. Our website is designed to provide live scores and football-related data without requiring user registration or personal data input.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous website usage data, such as page views, referral sources, and browsing patterns. This information helps us understand how our website is used and improve our services. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect this data, and you can opt-out by adjusting your browser settings.

Data Retention

Since we don't collect personal information, we don't retain any user data.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to ensure our website's security, including using HTTPS encryption (SSL) and regular security updates.

GDPR Compliance

We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) by:

  • Not collecting personal data
  • Using anonymous analytics tools
  • Providing this privacy policy

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice on our website.

Data Sources

We use third-party data providers to fetch football livescores and related data. These providers may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies. By using our website, you acknowledge that we may share anonymous usage data with our data providers to improve their services.

Data Provider Privacy Policies

Please find links to our data providers' privacy policies below:

  • https://www.api-football.com/privacy
  • https://allsportsapi.com/terms-of-use

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us at support@720scores.com.ng
